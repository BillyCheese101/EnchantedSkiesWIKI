  • Elements actually do stuff!

    There are four elements Ignistran (Fire), Aquarius (Water), Terravus (Earth)and Caelius (Air).

    Elements now have a purpose, not only do they have cool names and colours they are incorporated in the new custom crafting and pets systems.

    Each element gets 3 unique pets.

    Fire: Pigman, Blaze and Magmacube

    Water: Silverfish, squid and guardian

    Earth: Cow, slime and creeper

    Air: Chicken, bat and enderman

  • The spawners are tiered as to how difficult it is for their grinder to be made

    Tier 1(FREE) - Snow Golem(2k), Cave spider(3k), Zombie(1k)

    Tier 2(40K) - Sheep(10k), Silverfish(15k), Blaze(8k)

    Tier 3(300k) - Cow(40k), Slime(40k), Endermite(55k)

    Tier 4(800k) -Creeper(100k), Mooshroom(170k), Guardian(200k)

    Tier 5(2.5mill) - Squid(300k), Iron Golem(500k) , Ghast(800k)
