Getting an Element

When you first join the server you need to pick an Element

Runes are used to buy many things. You can get them every hour, from /kits and from envoy. You can buy pets, keys, trails and sell wands. To see the different things do /shop and go to the certain one you want to go to.

  • Elements actually do stuff!

    There are four elements Ignistran (Fire), Aquarius (Water), Terravus (Earth)and Caelius (Air).

    Elements now have a purpose, not only do they have cool names and colours they are incorporated in the new custom crafting and pets systems.

    Each element gets 3 unique pets.

    Fire: Pigman, Blaze and Magmacube

    Water: Silverfish, squid and guardian

    Earth: Cow, slime and creeper

    Air: Chicken, bat and enderman

  • New and improved envoys...

    Envoys.. but better

    Every hour a magical event called a Raid is triggered. This event involves caskets of varying rarity, Common Ancient Divine, drop around /warp pvp offering great riches of armour, money and more. Players can fight to the death to win the awesome loot, but beware you may lose everything.

  • Your new best friend!

    There are 4 types of pets:

    Basic: Pig, Rabbit, Wolf, Cat

    Advanced: Spider, Skeleton, Zombie

    Specialist: Villager, Iron Golem, Witch, Endermite. (These pets have special powers that will help you dominate :) )

    Seasonal/Exclusive: Seasonal pets eg: Halloween and Christmas. Exclusive pets can be bought from the store.

    Commands:

    /pcst Choose a skill tree. If you choose riding you have to get a lead or pet rider to ride it.

    /pc Call your pet to you

    /psw Change your pet

    /petname (name) Give your pet a snazzy new name (CheesyPuffs)

    /petstore Store ya pet

    /pb To change its mode (aggressive, passive, dueling ect.)

    /petinfo To see your pets info

    /petskill To see your pets statsss.

Elemental Crafting

Fire: When blocking with the weapon and an arrow hits you, a flaming arrow is sent back at your attacker, smoldering them!

Water: A diamond axe that does 'pure damage', this damage ignores potion effects and armour, additionally on a cooldown lightning is summoned from the gods to smite your enemy!

Earth: A diamond shovel that tosses your attacker into the air and gives them nausea and weakness, truely disorienting.

Air: A magical iron sword which can be used to throw a magical pearl that you ride into battle on!
